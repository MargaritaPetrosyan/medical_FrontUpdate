Mamoplasty

General information

Tender skin of breasts in time over-stretches and gradually loses its elasticity. This has adverse effect for shape and look of the breast. Pregnancy and breastfeeding, lifestyle and food also leave their negative trace. Even commitment to certain type of clothes and smoking may cause reduction of breast (its “sagging”) and to have negative effect on its elasticity. If this is the case, your choice shall be mammoplasty and its most common type – breast augmentation. Plastic surgery (lifting) is recommended to patients with nipples and areolas located on the lower breast line and pointing down. It is with mentioning, that patients with large breast also may carry out rejuvenating uplift, yet, they need to take into consideration, that the results of such procedure will remain in effect for a shorter period, then in case of small or medium breast. Breast uplift, plastic surgery and augmentation are methods to change shape or size of breast with application of surgical intervention. It has to be noted, that today breast augmentation is one of the most required types of plastic surgeries.

Pre-surgical preparation

At the first consultation, the doctor-surgeon answers all the patient's questions in detail, talks about the expected results and warns about possible complications. Before breast plastic surgery, it is required:
• give a number of analyzes (blood from a finger, biochemistry, correlogram, AIDS, hepatitis, etc.);
• undergo medical procedures (fluorography, USM, ECG);
• consultation with relevant field specialists (therapist, gynecologist, mammologist).
• A few days before the appointed time, it is recommended to keep a light diet, to give up alcohol and smoking.

Rehabilitation period

It is required to approach the end stage after the operation with all seriousness, properly following the recommendations of the specialist, coming to the examination on time, and taking the prescribed medicines. It usually takes about 6-9 months for the chest to take its full shape. In order to avoid complications and possible complications, it is required: Clean them with an antiseptic until they are removed or absorbed; 1-2 months to wear special compression underwear that keeps the chest in the right position and does not allow the implants to interfere; Avoid going to the bath, sauna, solarium, SPA salon, swimming pool for 4-6 weeks; Limiting physical exertion for two months. It is not recommended to raise the arms for the first few days. Unpleasant side effects such as pain, swelling, and stiffness can be observed at first after breast plastic surgery. Following all the recommendations of the surgeon, these factors will disappear.
